## Getting Started

1. **Open the app**: Simply open `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Fill the form**: Enter your feature goal, target users, and any constraints
3. **Generate**: Click "Generate Tasks" to create your user stories and engineering tasks
4. **Edit & Organize**: Customize the generated tasks to fit your needs
5. **Export**: Copy or download your specification

## Usage

### Creating a New Specification

1. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Goal**: What you want to achieve with this feature
   - **Target Users**: Who will use this feature
   - **Constraints**: Any technical or business limitations (optional)

2. Click **Generate Tasks** to create your specification
